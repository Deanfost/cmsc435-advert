Video Chat and Breakout Rooms

Introduction

In the Fall 2021 semester, our team of 7 people in CMSC 435 worked to add video chat and breakout rooms to the existing SEAM Lab project of Decidio. Decidio is designed to be the ultimate decision making tool for organizations and teams of any size. The existing functionality in Decidio included the ability to create events and meetings within those events. These meeting types include things like individual input, group input, individual ranking, and group ranking. Obviously in order for Decidio to work practically as a team an existing software would need to be relied on such as Zoom or by using Decidio in person. We we sought to remedy this by adding in video chat and breakout rooms. This leads to a considerably better user experience as now the entire decision making process can be done within Decidio itself. Our additions go a long way in fulfilling the goal of making Decidio the ultimate decision making tool.
